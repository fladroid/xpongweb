Beam

The beam shows where the ball is going by pure physics — up to the first wall, paddle or goal. It stops where certainty ends: it never predicts the bounce or anyone's move.

Heatmap

The heatmap is a shadow, not a mind: it records only where the ball has physically crossed each goal line. Thin frames mark the bands on each wall; a band grows darker the more goals have landed in it. Counts accumulate through the session — Reset clears them. Toggle it with the Heatmap switch or the h key.

Navigation

Left player: W up, S down. Right player: O up, L down. On a touch device, drag on your own half of the field to move your paddle.
